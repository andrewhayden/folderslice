Where did my Gadget(s) go?!
FolderSlice has resized itself. Because it was at the edge of your Sidebar, Windows Vista has moved it (and perhaps some of your other gadgets) to a different panel in the Sidebar.

At the top of your Sidebar you should see a "+" button as well as arrows pointing left and right. You can use the left and right arrows to visit the other panels in the Sidebar and locate FolderSlice.
Don't show again (till Sidebar restarts)
